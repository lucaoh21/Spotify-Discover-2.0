Welcome to Discover Daily!

Discover songs based on artists and songs you love!

Inspired by Spotify's Discover Weekly playlists, this website was designed to give Spotify users more versatility with their music. This website allows users to see their top tracks and artists, and can create playlists based off a user's preferences (in the hope of creating a Discover Daily playlist).
